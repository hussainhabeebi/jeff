FAQ

Booking Questions

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?
+

Walk-ins are always welcome at Sahara Medical Centre. However, booking ahead via WhatsApp or phone ensures minimal waiting time and guarantees your preferred doctor is available.

How quickly can I get an appointment?
+

Same-day appointments are usually available. WhatsApp us at +971 50 882 1590 and we'll confirm your slot within minutes.

What should I bring to my appointment?
+

Please bring your Emirates ID and any relevant insurance card. For dental appointments, bring previous X-rays if available. For follow-up visits, bring your previous prescription or medical reports.

Can I book for a family member?
+

Yes. You can book for yourself or any family member. Just mention the patient's name and service when you WhatsApp or call us.

Is the availability calendar real-time?
+

The calendar shows typical weekly schedules for each doctor. Actual slot availability is confirmed via WhatsApp instantly — just send us your preferred time after selecting a doctor.

Do you accept insurance?
+

Yes, we accept most major UAE insurance cards. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment. You can also call us ahead to verify your specific insurance coverage.
