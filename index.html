About Dr. Jeff Zacharia

Dr. Jeff Zacharia is an Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 5 years of clinical experience in managing a wide range of surgical conditions affecting the oral and facial region. He is known for his precise technique, patient-focused approach, and emphasis on minimizing discomfort and recovery time. His clinical interests include wisdom tooth removal, TMJ disorders, and oral pathology.

Dr. Jeff is committed to delivering safe, effective, and comfortable surgical care with clear communication and individualized treatment planning.
